Introduction

NaNa Media was a concept project focused on creating a modern and user-friendly platform for manga sales. The goal was to integrate intuitive browsing, a wishlist system, and clean branding that resonates with younger audiences.

Disciplines

  • UX Research
  • UI Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Branding

Responsibilities

  • User testing & feedback analysis
  • Wireframing & prototyping
  • Brand creation & logo design
  • Final visual design & mockups

Brand Research

The visual and functional inspiration came from existing streaming and e-commerce platforms. I studied apps like Crunchyroll, Spotify, and Amazon to balance entertainment design with efficient shopping flow.
